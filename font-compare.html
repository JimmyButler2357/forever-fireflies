Font Comparison

The app uses two fonts. Serif (changes per card) is the "journal voice" — titles, transcripts, the parent's actual words. Sans-serif (stays the same in every card) is the "app voice" — buttons, labels, child names, timestamps, tags, navigation. On the entry cards below, the child name/date row is sans-serif and the preview text is serif.
