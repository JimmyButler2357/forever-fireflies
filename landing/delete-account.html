Delete Your Account

If you'd like to permanently delete your Forever Fireflies account and all associated data, you can do so from within the app or by contacting us below.

What Gets Deleted

  • All audio recordings
  • All text entries and memories
  • Children's names, birthdays, and profiles
  • Family settings and preferences
  • Your account and login credentials
  • Push notification tokens and history

Delete from the App

The fastest way to delete your account is directly in the app:

  1. Open Forever Fireflies
  2. Go to Settings
  3. Scroll to the bottom and tap Delete Account
  4. Confirm when prompted

Your account and all data will be deleted immediately.

Already Uninstalled?

If you've already uninstalled the app or can't access it, you can request account deletion by email.

Important: For your security, you must send the request from the same email address you used to create your Forever Fireflies account. This is how we verify you're the account owner. Requests sent from a different email address will not be processed.

We'll process verified requests within 48 hours and send a confirmation when your account has been deleted.

About Subscriptions

Deleting your account does not automatically cancel your subscription. If you have an active subscription, you'll need to cancel it separately:

  • iPhone / iPad: Settings → [your name] → Subscriptions
  • Android: Google Play Store → Payments & subscriptions → Subscriptions

Data Retention

When your account is deleted, all data is permanently removed. We do not retain any of your personal data, entries, recordings, or children's information after deletion.

Anonymous, aggregated analytics data (which cannot identify you) may remain in our analytics systems per their standard retention policies.

For more details, see our Privacy Policy.
